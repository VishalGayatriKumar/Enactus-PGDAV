Vision

Enactus PGDAV is determined to uplift and empower those people, who due to poverty, helplessness, disability or any other social or economic disadvantage, could not enter into mainstream society. We aim to achieve global goals through sustainable, smart business solutions that can help community members and provide them with better means of livelihood. Through our economically, socially and environmentally sustainable business models, we aspire to help in creating a better future.
