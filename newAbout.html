What is Enactus?

Enactus is the world’s largest experiential learning platform dedicated to creating a better world while developing the next generation of entrepreneurial leaders and social innovators. The Enactus network of global business, academic and student leaders are unified by our vision—to create a better, more sustainable world.

At Enactus, we believe investing in students who take entrepreneurial action for others creates a better world for us all.

Enactus PGDAV

Enactus is an international non-profit organization dedicated to inspiring students to improve the world through entrepreneurial action.

Enactus PGDAV is currently running 4 ventures by the name of Project Nistaaran , Project Sugandh , Project Korakagaz and Project Hope. In the year 2020 it stood as one of the three winners in ‘Enactus National Competition 2020’ in the ‘New League Category’. Through its entrepreneurial action, it believes in empowering waste pickers and women of marginalised communities along with creating a positive environmental impact.
