What is Project Nistaaran?

Project Nistaaran aims to empower the ragpickers both socially and economically by providing them with the skills and network required to produce and market organic manure. This manure is produced using the domestic waste collected from the residential colonies of South Delhi. The revenue generated from the sales of the manure acts as an additional source of income for the wastepickers. Presently, we are working with 30 waste pickers from Defence Colony area of South Delhi. We aim to make these wastepickers self reliant by giving them an opportunity to have an alternative source of income. We also aim to spread awareness amongst people about the importance of proper waste segregation practices. Our product's target market includes nurseries, schools, colleges and households
