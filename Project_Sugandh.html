What is Project Sugandh?

Project Sugandh is one of our new projects which aims to protect people from malicious health problems and nurture the environment by providing non toxic and eco friendly incense sticks, as an alternative to harmful charcoal based incense sticks. In the coming times, we aim to collaborate with women from nearby marginalized communities and empower them both economically and socially by providing them with the skills and network required to produce and market incense sticks.This will not only fulfill our aim of producing environment friendly incense sticks, but would also give an alternative source of income to the women associated with us. Our product's target market includes temples, households, spas and meditation centers.
